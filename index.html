How does technology

Help Society?

Technology is all around us,most people use a mobile device everyday to access various kinds of apps or websites, others use laptops,desktops, and other forms of technology. We use technology in various ways, like contacting a family member or friend, whether that be through a phone call or sending a quick text message. Other ways include, shopping,entertainment,and education. Tech can also be complex. Developers work to create apps or websites to help society, for example, when it comes to the weather, we can typically open up a weather app to check the weather or recieve emergency alerts due to severe climate conditions. Tech can help various people get to safety when it comes to weather realted issues.


Technology can be a source of tremendous optimism. It can help overcome some of the greatest challenges our society faces, including climate change, famine, and disease.
--Josephine Wolff UCPRESS

Technology also helps students and techers. Students can purchse e-books,access to various websites,communicate with teachers or other students. Teachers use technology to provide students with visual and interavtive learning, they are able to communicate with students or parents, they use technology to store useful information like grades, and use it to teach and share lessons while also teaching students how to improve technical skills especially students younger in age.
